Who We Are

A Foundation Built on Compassion

InAmigos Foundation is a registered non-profit organisation founded on September 23, 2020 by Govind Shukla, headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. Licensed by the Central Government under Section 8 of the Companies Act 2013, we work at the intersection of education, nutrition, women's empowerment, environmental care, animal welfare, and livelihood development.

Our growing network of 28+ dedicated volunteers operates across multiple states, driven by one unified mission: to serve every underprivileged individual with dignity and respect.

80G Certified 12A Certified CSR-1 Registered NITI Aayog ISO 9001:2015 Section 8 NGO
Our Initiatives

6 Projects. One Mission.

Each project is designed to address a critical social need — from hunger to education to environmental sustainability.

🍱
Project Seva
Food & Clothing Distribution

Tackling hunger and inadequate clothing among impoverished communities. Our volunteers conduct regular food drives, distribute essential clothing, and bring warmth to those living on the margins.

50,000+ meals & clothing items distributed
📚
Project Bachpanshala
Education for Rural Children

Empowering underprivileged children with quality education, digital literacy, and life skills. We nurture young dreams in rural areas, bridging educational gaps one classroom at a time.

Educating children across rural Chhattisgarh
🦋
Project Udaan
Women Empowerment

A flagship movement transforming lives through vocational training, skill development, and mentorship. Already active across Chhattisgarh, Haryana, and West Bengal — aiming for 10 more states by 2026.

900+ women trained · 250+ microenterprises launched
🌳
Project Prakriti
Environmental Protection

Organising tree-planting drives, clean-up campaigns, and conservation workshops. We engage local communities to build a sense of environmental responsibility for future generations.

Thousands of saplings planted across India
💼
Project Vikas
Skill Development & Employment

Facilitating internship training in data operations, finance, research, content writing, digital marketing, and social work — equipping youth with career-ready skills for the modern world.

30,000+ interns trained in 4 years
🐾
Project Jeev
Animal Welfare

Extending our compassion beyond humans to our animal companions. Project Jeev addresses animal welfare through rescue, feeding drives, and community awareness campaigns for coexistence and care.

Compassion for every living being

Our Purpose

Why We Do What We Do

Six pillars of action. One vision: a compassionate, inclusive, and sustainable India.

🎓

Education for All

No child should be denied the right to learn. We break barriers to education in rural and underserved communities across India.

♀️

Women's Dignity

Empowering women through skill training, mentorship, and micro-enterprise support to achieve financial independence and social equality.

🥣

Zero Hunger

Nourishing communities through regular food and clothing drives, ensuring no one sleeps hungry or without basic necessities.

🌿

Green Earth

Protecting our planet through tree-plantation drives, clean-up campaigns, and environmental education for the next generation.

🐕

Animal Welfare

Every life matters. We extend compassion to animals through rescue operations, feeding drives, and community awareness campaigns.

🤝

Youth Leadership

Training thousands of young Indians through internships and skill development programmes, building India's future workforce.
