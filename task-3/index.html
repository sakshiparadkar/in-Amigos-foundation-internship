Our Story

Built by fintech veterans, designed for builders

Founded in 2019 in Mumbai, NexaVault was born from frustration with fragmented financial tooling. Our founding team spent years at Goldman Sachs, Razorpay, and Stripe before deciding to build the platform they always wished existed.

Today, we power payments and treasury for thousands of businesses across 180+ countries — from seed-stage startups to Fortune 500 enterprises.
